MEV — Maximal Extractable Value — is one of the most important forces on Ethereum, and most people have never heard of it. In this first episode of a brand-new series, we explain MEV from the very beginning: what it is, where the term came from (the 2019 "Flash Boys 2.0" paper), and we prove why transaction order is worth money with a real Foundry test you can run yourself — then watch the original form of MEV, a Priority Gas Auction, play out on a local chain in a real block explorer.

Everything runs on free, open-source tools (Foundry's Anvil + Otterscan) on a private local fork — no exploits against real users.
